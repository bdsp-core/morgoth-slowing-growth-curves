# Severity & prevalence agreement vs report adjectives

Matched 12880 recordings with a report severity/frequency word.

- **Severity**: our peak-z vs report severity — Spearman ρ = 0.037 (n=5270); burden vs severity ρ = 0.041
- **Prevalence**: our %-segments vs report frequency — Spearman ρ = 0.121 (n=12188)
