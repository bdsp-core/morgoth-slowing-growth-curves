# Severity & prevalence agreement vs report adjectives

Matched 773 recordings with a report severity/frequency word.

- **Severity**: our peak-z vs report severity — Spearman ρ = -0.036 (n=222); burden vs severity ρ = -0.050
- **Prevalence**: our %-segments vs report frequency — Spearman ρ = 0.100 (n=640)
